Turritopsis nutricula

Turritopsis nutricula is a small hydrozoan that once reaching adulthood, can transfer its cells back to childhood.
  • Phylum: Cbudarua
  • Class: Hydrozoa
  • Order: Anthoathecata
  • Genus: Turritopsis

Turritopsis nutricula in any point of the medusa stage has the ability to transform back into its polyp stage. T. nutricula is the first known metazoan that has been observed to sexually mature and return to its juvenile colonial stage.
